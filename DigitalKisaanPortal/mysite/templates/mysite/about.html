ABOUT US

Live Analysis Crop Prediction and Recommendation System

Our Application is a Crop Prediction and Recommendation System using live crop growing factors such as Precipitation, Soil Moisture, Weather, Solar Radiation etc. which will help farmers to grow the right crop at the right time. It would also give predictions based on previous dataset available so as the farmer can compare the prediction and previous dataset approximations to take better decisions.

This application also includes a growing farmers-community where farmers can ask their questions and other colleagues and experts can answer the question which leads to a self rising community or discussion forum and can also chat with the experts in that particular field for more precise knowledge.

The application also gives the farmers a chance to post their success stories and share important technologies, innovations and information with the help of Live Feed Page which helps the farmers in socializing with the community.

Comprising the web application also supports a quick ChatBot for immediate queries and other help with the webpage. It also contains a page where farmers can see and would be notified regarding particular government subsidies in that particular state where they can learn the whole process for applying for the schemes they can be benifiteed from!
